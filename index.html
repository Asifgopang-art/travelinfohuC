Visa Requirements — Quick Start

Below are short, actionable checklists for common travel visas. Click a country for details and step-by-step documents required.

Schengen Visa — Short Checklist

For short stays (tourism, family visit). Typical documents:

  • Valid passport (≥3 months after return)
  • Completed application form
  • Two passport photos
  • Travel medical insurance (min. €30,000)
  • Flight reservation & hotel booking
  • Proof of funds & ties to home country

Read full Schengen guide

UK Visit Visa — Quick Checklist

  • Online visa application (UKVI)
  • Passport + photos
  • Proof of accommodation & purpose
  • Bank statements / proof of funds
  • TB test if required (depending on country)

USA Visitor Visa (B1/B2)

  • DS-160 online form
  • Visa appointment & consular interview
  • Passport valid for travel
  • Proof of ties & funds

Canada Visitor Visa

Travel Tips & Safety

Packing Smart

Pack light, keep copies of passport & documents, use TSA-approved locks, carry essential medicines in hand luggage.

Health & Insurance

Buy travel medical insurance with adequate coverage; check vaccine requirements before travel.

Money & Payments

Notify your bank for foreign travel, carry a small amount of local currency, prefer cards with low FX fees.

Frequently Asked

How long does a Schengen visa take?
Processing varies: typically 15 calendar days, but can be longer. Apply early.
Do I need travel insurance for visa?
Most Schengen and many countries require travel medical insurance. Always check the visa checklist.
